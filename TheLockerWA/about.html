About The Locker WA

At The Locker WA we believe all people can incorporate a high level of fitness and activity in their lives with safety and correct form being paramount. We are available to assist you in your journey to the best and fittest life possible.

We incorporate a variety of exercises that help people of all ages achieve a higher level of fitness.We believe that people young and old can better their life with our workouts.

Frank is, in short, a renaissance man. Having been a multi-sport athlete and scholar his whole life, he truly understands people from different walks of life (jocks and nerds alike). Harnessing this unique balance of muscle and brain power enables Frank to excel in all endeavors. Frank came from humble beginnings and learned early on that his path in life was going to be a bit more challenging than the normal. With hard work and dedication he earned an athletic scholarship to play football at the University of Southern California where he graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering and Computer Sciences. Frank consistently made the Dean's List and was a 2-time winner of the David X Marke scholar athlete award. Frank also earned Academic All-American and Special Teams Player of the Year honors.After graduating, Frank briefly pursued professional football but quickly realized his injuries were accumulating and his time was up so he began his professional career as a system/software developer. With drive, teamwork, and leadership Frank quickly earned lead developer positions, where he managed teams of developers for numerous projects for Fortune 500 companies. Leveraging his established reputation and network, Frank started his own software consulting business, ByteKnowledge LLC, which he continues to own and manage today. Being a former athlete, Frank always gravitated towards working out, playing sports, and reading up on the latest information in nutrition and exercise. This passion led him to CrossFit, where he began training and competing. Frank has obtained his ACSM personal training, Certified L1 Crossfit Trainer, and CPR/AED certifications. In his free time Frank enjoys playing with his son, reading biographies or philosophy, and avidly partakes in CrossFit-style workouts and any sports related activities.
